Telehealth Sessions

Telehealth for counseling sessions can offer safety and convenience, especially while health continues to be a concern. Envision Therapies continues to provide sessions through Doxy.me which provides secure connections. Sessions can be provided while the client remains in Washington State. Telehealth can sometimes provide more comfortable, more convenient sessions. Sessions can be shifted from in person to telehealth or from telehealth to in person conveniently. EMDR can be provided by telehealth -- It does require some extra preperation and extra considerations. There are some limitations to EMDR through telehealth that may require adjusting to in person sessions.

Sessions

Introduction Consultation

Counseling is a personal and often intimate experience. It's unlike many professional relationships -- it requires trust and safety. Please feel free to arrange a 30 minute consultation. You'll have a chance to make sure that sessions will beneficial. (And we can get some of the necessary paperwork out of the way when sessions do begin.)

Hourly Sessions

Sessions typically last 55 minutes. (Some insurances limit the time to 45 minutes.) Sessions can take on very different rhythms, styles, and direction. A cornerstone of the process is to building insight in order to create change. The opportunity to sit with another person, offers insight that other experiences cannot. The real change then comes between sessions.

Extended

For certain reasons, extended sessions may be helpful. Usually they're used for assessment and planning future sessions. When using EMDR longer sessions can also be more effective in completing the reprocessing protocol. Couples often appreciate the extra time that extended sessions provide. If extended sessions are necessary, I will plan them with you ahead of time.
